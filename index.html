Georgios Selimis

About

I am a cybersecurity and cryptography professional with expertise spanning security architecture, standardisation, regulatory compliance, and emerging security technologies. My work bridges technical, regulatory, and policy-driven perspectives, with a particular focus on cryptography, security protocols, trust infrastructures, and secure digital ecosystems.

I have over 20 years of experience working in international and multidisciplinary environments, collaborating with regulators, industry, academia, standardisation bodies, and certification stakeholders. I have led and mentored engineering teams and supervised PhD and Master's students from diverse backgrounds.

I hold a PhD (2008) in Cryptographic Design and diploma (2003) on Electrical and Computer Engineering from the University of Patras in Greece.
