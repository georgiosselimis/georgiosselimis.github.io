Georgios Selimis

About

Georgios Selimis (Ph.D. 2008) is a professional engineer working on the security of micro devices for ambient intelligence. His expertise includes applied cryptography, embedded systems security, Root-of-Trust, IoT to cloud secure connectivity, security evaluation/risk analysis, security requirements, architecture design.

He publishes and presents results on high impact conferences and journals. He has received his diploma (2003) and PhD (2008) in Electrical & Computer Engineering department of University of Patras, Greece. He was born and raised in Greece.
